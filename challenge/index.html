Why your last plan died in week two

It wasn't your discipline. It was the design. A gym membership is a building with equipment in it — nobody notices when you stop showing up. An app is a notification you learn to swipe away. A franchise "challenge" is a funnel with a countdown timer that resets for the next person.

What actually works is boring and old: a coach who knows your name, a group that expects you at 5:30, and numbers on a card that don't lie. That's what this is. It's what it's been at Acacia Park since 2005 — long enough that our first members' kids now train here.

Six weeks is enough time to prove it to yourself. That's the whole pitch.

The six weeks

Simple on purpose

45-minute sessions in the open air. Strength, conditioning, and mobility that scales to your level — whether you're 30 or 74. (Yes, 74. You'll meet Elizabeth.)

WEEK 01

Baseline

Day one, we measure: weight, inches, and a simple fitness benchmark. No judgment — just a starting line. Then you learn the rhythm: show up, work hard, go home proud.

WEEKS 02–05

Build

Three coached sessions a week, every workout different, every exercise adjusted to you. The nutrition guide is SoCal-simple: real food, no macros spreadsheet, no starving.

WEEK 06

Proof

Day 42, we re-measure everything from day one. You see the difference in numbers, in photos, and in how your clothes fit. Then you decide what's next — no pressure, no contract.
